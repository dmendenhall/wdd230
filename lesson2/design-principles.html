David Mendenhall

White Space & Clean Design

Board Game Geek

boardgamegeek.com

There is a lot of content on this site and so it can be very easy to get lost or to be able to distinguish one section from another; but they have done a good job of allowing plenty of white space to allow the visitor to focus on each section. They've added additional white space between sections to make it easier to differentiate one from the other and I believe their choice to leave most of the background white, especially behind the text, makes it appear cleaner and lends to an improved comprehension for the site visitor.

Repetition

Desert Star

desertstar.biz

The site is busy and has a lot of colors on it for each specific show, but they have done an excellent job in using and repeating the same pattern for each of their graphics. There is white space between sections to allow for an easy distinction and then each graphic has the name of the show in a large fun font with a smaller font below that provides additional context. They've also repeated the red banner at the bottom of the graphic with the dates of the show. Overall, the visitor to their site can establish a reasonable expectation as to what will be next because of the repetition within their site.

Proximity

Vudu

vudu.com

This site has a significant amount of graphics and is broken down into different sections to convey to the visitor what to expect. The way the site is laid out by grouping certain shows together and under a heading allows us to more easily make sense of the information and what to expect. It is the proximity of each graphic to the next that alerts us to the idea that they belong within the same group and by looking at the section title, I am able to determine what that is.
